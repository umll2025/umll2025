Camera-Ready Instruction

1. FINAL PAPER: Final paper should be prepared according to the Springer LNCS format and submitted using your EasyChair author account.Follow the instructions after the login for uploading two files:

  • either a zipped file containing all your LaTeX sources or a Word file in the RTF format
  • the PDF version of your camera-ready paper

The page limit is 8-10 Pages for Full Papers and 4-6 Pages for Short Papers. Please follow strcitly the author instructions of Springer-Verlag when preparing the final version: GUIDELINES.

2. COPYRIGHT: Please upload a signed and completed copyright form to us as soon as possible. The Springer copyright form template can be found at HERE. It is sufficient for one of the authors to sign the copyright form. You can scan the form into PDF or any other standard image format.
